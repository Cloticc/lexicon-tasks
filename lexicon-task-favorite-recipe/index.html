Swedish Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 135 grams of salted butter (EU = just over 1/2 deciliter or just over 1 knob of butter)
  • 60 grams of cocoa powder (plus 1 tablespoon) unsweetened, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 215 grams of sugar
  • 120 grams of plain flour (actually, just less than a full cup, about 0.85 deciliters)
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs lightly whisked
